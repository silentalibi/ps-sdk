Support

SDK Online Forum

Adobe Online Forums are for the Adobe community's peer-to-peer discussions of both Adobe and formerly Macromedia products. Please note that these forums are not an official customer support channel for Adobe. If you require direct assistance or prefer to contact Support directly, please use our paid support programs or complimentary product support for registered users.

To access the Photoshop SDK Online Forum, follow these instructions :

  1. Go to Adobe Support Forums
  2. Click on "Photoshop"
  3. In the Forums list in the middle of the page click on "Photoshop Plugin and Companion App SDK"
